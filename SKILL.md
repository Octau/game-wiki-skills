extracted from the official wiki (milkywayidle.wiki.gg). Use it to answer player
questions accurately across all skill levels — from new players to min-maxers.

## How to use this skill

1. Identify which area the question touches (gathering, production, combat,
   monsters, houses, etc.).
2. Read the matching reference file below **before answering** — do not rely on
   memory for numbers (drop rates, XP values, upgrade costs, success-rate
   tables, stat formulas). These are the parts most likely to be misremembered.
3. Quote specific figures from the reference. If a value isn't in the references,
   say so rather than guessing.

- This data is a **snapshot** of the wiki and the game is actively updated.
  Balance numbers (drop rates, costs, formulas, item stats) can change. For
  anything where currentness is critical, note that the live wiki or in-game

## Quick orientation (game basics)

Milky Way Idle has many **Skills** to level and **Items** to collect. Skills split
into gathering (Milking, Foraging, Woodcutting), production/artisan (Cheesesmithing,
Crafting, Tailoring, Cooking, Brewing, Alchemy), Enhancing, and Combat (which
contains the sub-skills Stamina, Intelligence, Attack, Defense and the damage
styles Melee, Ranged, Magic). Combat yields loot used for armor, enhancing, and
alchemy. Houses provide per-skill and global buffs. Teas (skilling) and
coffees (combat) are temporary consumable buffs. There's a player Marketplace,
Tasks, Guilds, and Loadouts. The advice the wiki gives newcomers: find what you
enjoy and have fun.
