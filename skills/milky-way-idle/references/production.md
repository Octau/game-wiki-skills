## Cheesesmithing
================================================================

Cheesesmithing

Did you know you can make equipment using these special hardened cheeses?

Main Drop

Cheese

Tool

Hammer

**Cheesesmithing** is a production skill in Milky Way Idle. The player can gain Experience to level up this skill by converting milk into cheese and crafting equipment from cheese. There are seven tiers of cheese that the player can unlock by leveling up Cheesesmithing. The time it takes to craft increases accordingly to the tier and complexity of items. The player can reduce the time it takes to craft by using Hammers. Various pieces of Equipment can boost Cheesesmithing efficiency, experience gain, and rare find.

Cheese has two main uses: crafting equipment (weapons, armor, and tools for all skills) and serving as a material in various Cooking and Brewing recipes.

## Formulas

### Action Time

The time to craft cheese or equipment is calculated as:

| Action Time Formula |
| --- |
| `Action Time = Base Time / (1 + Cheesesmithing Speed)` |

**Cheesesmithing Speed Sources:**

| Source | Bonus |
| --- | --- |
| Hammers | +15% to +105% (base values, scales with enhancement) |
| Necklace Of Speed | +4% base (9.8% at +10) |
| Philosopher's Necklace | +4% base (9.8% at +10) |

**Example:**

| Component | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Base Time (Cheese) | 6 seconds |
| Azure Hammer Speed | +30% |
| **Final Action Time** | **6 / (1 + 0.30) = 4.62 seconds** |

### Experience

Wisdom and equipment experience bonuses increase the experience you receive from skilling actions. All Wisdom sources are additive with each other, then multiply the base experience.

| Skilling Experience Formula |
| --- |
| `Final XP = Base XP × (1 + Wisdom + Equipment Experience + Charm Experience)` |

**Cheesesmithing-Specific Equipment:**  Cheesemaker's Bottoms (Lv 90, +4% base (5.16% at +10)),  Celestial Hammer (Lv 90, +4% base (5.16% at +10)), Cheesesmithing Charm (scales with tier and enhancement)

See the Wisdom page for all sources and detailed examples.

### Efficiency

Efficiency allows you to instantly repeat actions without consuming time. At higher Efficiency percentages, you get guaranteed repeats plus a chance for additional actions.

See the Efficiency page for the full formula, all sources, and examples.

Crafting

Create weapons, jewelry, and more.

Main Drop

Lumber

Tool

**Crafting** is a production skill in Milky Way Idle. The player can gain Experience to level up this skill by converting logs into lumber and creating weapons, accessories, and specialized items. The player can reduce the time it takes to craft by using Chisels. Various pieces of Equipment can boost Crafting efficiency, experience gain, and rare find.

Crafted items include lumber (used in other skills), weapons (bows, crossbows, staves, shields), accessories (rings, earrings, necklaces), skill charms, trinkets, and special materials.

## Formulas

### Action Time

The time to craft items is calculated as:

| Action Time Formula |
| --- |
| `Action Time = Base Time / (1 + Crafting Speed)` |

**Crafting Speed Sources:**

| Source | Bonus |
| --- | --- |
| Chisels | +15% to +105% (base values, scales with enhancement) |
| Necklace Of Speed | +4% base (9.8% at +10) |
| Philosopher's Necklace | +4% base (9.8% at +10) |

**Example:**

| Component | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Base Time (Lumber) | 6 seconds |
| Azure Chisel Speed | +30% |
| **Final Action Time** | **6 / (1 + 0.30) = 4.62 seconds** |

### Experience

Wisdom and equipment experience bonuses increase the experience you receive from skilling actions. All experience bonuses are additive with each other, then multiply the base experience.

| Skilling Experience Formula |
| --- |
| `Final XP = Base XP × (1 + Wisdom + Equipment Experience + Charm Experience)` |

**Crafting-Specific Equipment:**  Crafter's Bottoms (Lv 90, +4% base, 5.16% at +10),  Celestial Chisel (Lv 90, +4% base, 5.16% at +10), Crafting Charm (+1% to +8% based on tier)

See the Wisdom page for all sources and detailed examples.

## Tailoring
================================================================

Tailoring

Create ranged and magic clothing.

Main Drop

Leather, Fabric

Tool

Needle

**Tailoring** is a production skill in Milky Way Idle. The player can gain Experience to level up this skill by converting hides and fibers into leather and fabric, and crafting equipment from these materials. There are five tiers of leather and fabric that the player can unlock by leveling up Tailoring. The time it takes to craft increases accordingly to the tier and complexity of items. The player can reduce the time it takes to craft by using Needles. Various pieces of Equipment can boost Tailoring efficiency, experience gain, and rare find.

Tailored items include leather and fabric (used in Tailoring recipes and other skills), ranged equipment (hoods, tunics, chaps, bracers), magic equipment (hats, robe tops, robe bottoms, gloves), skilling gear for all non-combat skills, and pouches that provide HP/MP bonuses and additional food and Drink Slots.

**Cooking** is a production skill in Milky Way Idle. The player can gain Experience to level up this skill by creating consumable foods that restore HP and MP. There are seven tiers of recipes that the player can unlock by leveling up Cooking. The time it takes to cook increases accordingly to the tier of recipes. Cooked foods are consumables used in Combat to restore HP and MP during battles. Each food type serves a different purpose: instant healing, healing over time, instant mana restoration, or mana regeneration over time.

**Alchemy** is a production skill that allows players to transform equipment, consumables, and materials through three distinct processes: Coinify (convert items to gold), Decompose (break items into components), and Transmute (convert items into related items). Success rates depend on your Alchemy level relative to the item's level, with Catalytic Tea and catalysts improving your chances.

## Actions

Alchemy has three distinct actions, each with different base success rates and outputs:

| Action | Base Success Rate | Cost | Output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Coinify** | 70% | Item consumed | 5× vendor sell price in  Coin |
| **Decompose** | 60% | Item + gold | Crafting materials or essence |
| **Transmute** | Varies by item | Item + gold | Different item from same family or rare items |

**On Failure:** The item is destroyed and you receive 10% of the base experience.

**On Success:** You receive full experience, the output items, and any catalysts used are consumed.

**Note:** Trainee-tier charms cannot be coinified and must be upgraded or sold to vendors.

### Success Rate

Success rates depend on your Alchemy level relative to the Item Level, with catalyst bonuses and level caps.

| Alchemy Success Rate Formula |
| --- |
| `If Alchemy Level < Item Level: Final Success Rate = Base Rate × Catalyst Multiplier × (1 + Per Level × (Alchemy Level - Item Level))` |
| `If Alchemy Level ≥ Item Level: Final Success Rate = Base Rate × Catalyst Multiplier` |

**Components:**

**Base Rate:** The base success rate for the alchemy operation:

* Coinify: 70%
* Decompose: 60%
* Transmute: Varies by item (50%-80%)

**Catalyst:** Multiplicative bonus from catalyst items (consumed only on success):

* Catalyst Of Coinification: ×1.15 (Coinify only)
* Catalyst Of Decomposition: ×1.15 (Decompose only)
* Catalyst Of Transmutation: ×1.15 (Transmute only)
* Prime Catalyst: ×1.25 (Any action)
* Catalytic Tea: +5% (Any action)

**Per Level:** `0.9 ÷ Item Level`

**Level Difference:** `(Alchemy Level - Item Level)`

**Example (Below Item Level):**

* Alchemy Level: 30, Item Level: 40 ( Amethyst)
* Base Rate: 55%, Catalyst: None (1.0× multiplier)

| Component | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Base Rate | 55.0% |
| Catalyst Multiplier | 1.0 (no catalyst) |
| Per Level | 0.9 ÷ 40 = 0.0225 |
| Level Difference | 30 - 40 = -10 |
| Level Modifier | 1 + (0.0225 × -10) = 1 - 0.225 = 0.775 |
| **Final Success Rate** | **55.0% × 1.0 × 0.775 = 42.625%** |

**Example (At/Above Item Level):**

* Alchemy Level: 60, Item Level: 40 ( Amethyst)
* Base Rate: 55%, Catalyst: 1.15× multiplier ( Catalyst Of Transmutation)

| Component | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Base Rate | 55.0% |
| Catalyst Multiplier | 1.15 (15% multiplicative bonus) |
| **Final Success Rate** | **55.0% × 1.15 = 63.25%** |

**Important Notes:**

* Success rates are capped when at/above item level - no bonus for over-leveling
* Levels above item level contribute to Efficiency instead of success rate
* Catalysts are consumed only on successful attempts
