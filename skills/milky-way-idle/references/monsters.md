Monsters are enemies found in Combat that fight players. Every monster has its unique appearance and stats, appearing in Zones. Monsters are the main source of loot such as Tea Leaves, Combat Essences, and rare crafting materials.

### Zones

All monsters are sorted into categories known as Zones, which have themes defining the monsters within. All regular monsters can be fought solo or in Zone battles, both of which have their benefits.

### Bosses

Some monsters are much more powerful than others in their Zone and are known as bosses. Bosses cannot be fought solo and only appear in Zone battles every ten battles. Each Zone has its boss monster except Smelly Planet.

### Combat Tiers

Each monster and Zone has different difficulty levels, or tiers, ranging from Tier 0 (T0) to Tier 5 (T5). As you progress through tiers, monsters become progressively more challenging but offer greater rewards.

Increasing tiers has the following effect on the fights:

* Stronger Stats: Monsters gain increased HP, damage output, accuracy, evasion, and resistances with each tier
* Better Experience: Higher tiers provide more combat experience per kill
* Improved Drop Rates: Drop rates for rare items, key fragments, and treasure chests increase significantly at higher tiers
* More Resources: Common drop rates (essences, materials, coins) improve by approximately 10% per tier.
