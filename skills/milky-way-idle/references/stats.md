In Milky Way Idle, **stats** are numerical values from equipment that affect your combat capability and skilling efficiency. **Buffs** are temporary effects from consumables like food, drinks, and coffee.

## Combat Equipment Stats

### Offense

| Stat | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Damage | Base damage dealt by attacks and abilities |
| Accuracy | Chance to land an attack successfully |
| Attack Speed | Speed of auto attacks (inverse of attackInterval) |
| Cast Speed | Reduces time to cast Abilities |
| Ability Haste | Reduces ability cooldown |
| Critical Rate | Bonus chance to land a critical hit |
| Critical Damage | Increases damage dealt when landing a critical hit |
| Physical Amplify | Increases physical damage by a fixed percentage |
| Fire Amplify | Increases fire damage by a fixed percentage |
| Water Amplify | Increases water damage by a fixed percentage |
| Nature Amplify | Increases nature damage by a fixed percentage |
| Armor Penetration | Reduces enemy Armor when calculating damage |
| Fire Penetration | Reduces enemy fire resistance when calculating damage |
| Water Penetration | Reduces enemy water resistance when calculating damage |
| Nature Penetration | Reduces enemy nature resistance when calculating damage |
| Life Steal | Heals HP for a percentage of auto attack damage |
| Mana Leech | Recovers MP for a percentage of auto attack damage |
| Threat | Chance of being targeted by enemies in group combat |
